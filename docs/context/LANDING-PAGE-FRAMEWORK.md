# High-Converting Landing Page Anatomy
*Source: @oliverkenyon — 13 years CRO, 4,000+ brands, 8,000+ pages*
*Saved: April 16, 2026*

Works for: physical products, digital courses, SaaS, services. All follow the same structure.

---

## The 12-Section Framework

### 1. Above the Fold (Hero + Proof Bar)
- Headline
- Subheadline
- CTA
- Social proof bar (logos, reviews, trust badges)

**Rule:** Prove credibility immediately. Attention → Trust → Action — all above fold.

### 2. The Transformation (Process Steps)
- Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3
- Answers: "How easy is this going to be?"
- Keep it simple — if the process feels heavy, conversion drops

### 3. Benefits (Deep, Visual, Outcome-Led)
- Alternating: visual (image/graphic) + headline + supporting proof
- **Outcomes, not features**
- ❌ "High quality materials"
- ✅ "Built to last 10+ years. Tested across 5,000+ uses."
- Specificity = trust. Trust = conversion.

### 4. Features (Scannable Layer)
- Short features with icons, one-liners
- For people who won't read everything (most users)
- Let them skim their way to conviction

### 5. Mid-Page CTA + Proof
- Don't wait until the bottom — repeat the CTA mid-page
- Add proof again here (reviews, stats)

### 6. Social Proof (Deep Dive)
- Real reviews, real results
- Specifics beat generics every time
- Volume + specificity = credibility

### 7. Guarantee / Risk Reversal
- Remove the last reason to say no
- Money-back, free trial, satisfaction guarantee
- The stronger the guarantee, the higher the conversion

### 8. Comparison / Objection Handling
- "Why you vs. the alternative?"
- Answer it directly — don't avoid it
- Best brands lean into this

### 9. Authority / Who This Is For
- Credentials, case studies, results
- Clarity on ideal customer: "This is for you if..."

### 10. FAQ
- Not filler — it's objection handling
- Every question = a doubt, hesitation, or reason someone didn't buy
- Cover: shipping, returns, use case, compatibility, pricing

### 11. Image Strip / Product Visuals
- Right before the close
- Different angles, lifestyle shots, in-use moments
- Reinforces desire at the right moment

### 12. Final CTA (Emotive Close)
- Emotive headline (not generic)
- Clear CTA button
- Trust badges underneath: guarantees, payments, security
- Reduces final hesitation

## The Psychology Behind the Flow

- **Top:** Trust
- **Middle:** Belief
- **Bottom:** Decision

Conversion comes from:
- **What** you say
- **Where** you say it
- **When** you say it

Not from colors, fonts, or animations.

---

## Checklist for Our Pages

Apply to: Booked Out landing page, Chalice website, ICDC consulting page, any client pages

- [ ] Hero has headline + subheadline + CTA + proof bar above fold
- [ ] Transformation section shows simple 3-step process
- [ ] Benefits are outcome-led with visuals (not feature lists)
- [ ] Scannable features section with icons
- [ ] Mid-page CTA with proof
- [ ] Deep social proof section
- [ ] Guarantee / risk reversal
- [ ] Comparison section addressing "why us"
- [ ] FAQ covering all objections
- [ ] Final emotive CTA with trust badges
